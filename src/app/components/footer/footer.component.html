About Us

ApnaRozgar is an online job board for blue-collar jobs. The platform allows job seekers to create a profile by mentioning skills, education, experience, view jobs, call the employer directly, fix interview, and get hired. It allows employers to post jobs, communicate with candidates, schedule interviews, and hire. Notable clients include Byju's, Amazon, Toppr, Flipkart, Swiggy, WhitehatJr, Unacademy, BigBasket, Licious, etc.
